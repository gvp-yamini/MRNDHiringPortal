Welcome to Mission RnD.

While more than 300,000 engineers graduate in computer science and related fields each year from about 3500 engineering colleges, many are unemployable, and a very small number quality for R&D roles. There are some fundamental issues with our current engineering education model. Static curriculum, theory oriented approach, exam-centric learning model is resulting in students graduating with poor knowledge of fundamentals and lack of preparation for real world software development roles. Mission R&D believes that there are many intelligent, hard-working, motivated students in these colleges who can be transformed into world class R&D engineers with the right training.

     Mission R&D is an innovative initiative with the mission to “Contribute to the growth of India’s R&D ecosystem by developing the most promising technology students, especially women, and by supporting the talent needs of product companies including start-ups". Started by people who have many years of global product development experience, Mission R&D aims to transform the students through a short duration summer program and put them on a path towards a rewarding R&D career. The program helps the students gain expertise in computer science fundamentals, professional software development tools and latest programming platforms, gives them confidence and unleashes their inherent potential to become top R&D engineers.

     Mission R&D teaches the students Learn how to Learn. Change is constant, especially in software industry, and this is one of the most important skills that enables the individual to have a long successful career.
